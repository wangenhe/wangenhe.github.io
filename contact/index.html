你好，因为不知道你是谁又来自哪里，也不知道你此时在哪个世纪，从哪里进入了这个地址，以防万一我将做一个简短明了的自我介绍。
这里是银河系的地球，我是一个人类，地球的生物之一，这里最善于将智慧运用到科技工具上的生物。地球由陆地和海洋组成，如果你来自外星，应该在太空看到了地球的大概形态。不知道色彩在我们的概念里是否一样，从外太空看地球，最多的部分就是海洋，人类看来是蓝色。其余部分是陆地，人类看来是褐色和绿色————我们在陆地上生活了很久。

Hello, since I do not know who you are or where you come from, nor which century you might be in as you arrive at this place, I shall make a brief and clear introduction, just in case.

This is Earth, in the Milky Way galaxy. I am a human, one of the living beings here—the species most skilled at applying intellect to technological tools. Earth is made up of land and oceans. If you are from another galaxy, you may have already glimpsed Earth’s general shape from space. I wonder if our sense of color is the same as yours. From space, Earth’s most dominant feature is the ocean, which appears blue to us humans. The rest is land, seen by us as shades of brown and green—we have lived on this land for a long time.
